Be passionate. Be open-minded.

Social media and news sites today too often expose us to viewpoints we are likely to agree with. In order to understand the bigger picture, we must learn from each other. This site uses Artificial Intelligence neural networks to analyze and categorize user sentiments in order to help expose you to the voices and stories that often become lost in the increasingly more polarized world we live in today.

Share and support your views, but do not antagonize those with different views. Instead of saying "you're wrong, I'm right," ask: "why do you feel that way?" or "what experiences have you had that lead you to believe in what you do?"
